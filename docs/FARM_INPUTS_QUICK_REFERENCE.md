## What to Buy for Each Crop

### MAIZE (8,500 KSh/ha)
- **Fertilizer:** NPK 17:17:17 (250-300 kg) at planting
- **Top Dress:** CAN (150-200 kg) at 4-6 weeks
- **Pesticide:** If pests appear - Carbofuran or Cypermethrin
- **Seed:** 20-25 kg/ha improved seed
- **Manure:** 10-15 tons/ha farmyard manure

**Budget Save:** Skip pesticides if no pests visible

---

### BEANS (4,500 KSh/ha)
- **Fertilizer:** DAP (200-250 kg) at planting
- **Top Dress:** CAN (100-150 kg) at 4-6 weeks
- **Pesticide:** Mancozeb (every 10-14 days if rust appears)
- **Seed:** 30-40 kg/ha improved seed
- **Manure:** 8-10 tons/ha

**Budget Save:** Use manure for half the nitrogen

---

### TOMATOES (12,000 KSh/ha) - HIGH VALUE
- **Fertilizer 1:** NPK 17:17:17 (300-400 kg)
- **Fertilizer 2:** CAN (200-250 kg) in splits
- **Fertilizer 3:** Potassium Sulphate (150-200 kg)
- **Pesticide 1:** Mancozeb (every 7-10 days)
- **Pesticide 2:** Cypermethrin (every 10-14 days)
- **Seedlings:** 30,000-40,000
- **Stakes:** 15,000-20,000 pieces
- **String:** 50-100 kg for tying

**Budget Save:** Skip second pesticide if budget tight

---

### GROUNDNUTS (5,000 KSh/ha)
- **Fertilizer:** SSP (200-250 kg) at planting
- **Soil:** Lime (1-2 tons) before planting
- **Pesticide:** Mancozeb (every 10-14 days)
- **Seed:** 25-30 kg/ha
- **Manure:** 8-10 tons/ha

**Budget Save:** Skip pesticide if no disease visible

---

### CASSAVA (4,000 KSh/ha) - CHEAPEST!
- **Cuttings:** 20,000-25,000 pieces
- **Manure:** 8-12 tons/ha
- **Mulch:** 5-8 tons farm waste

**Why Cheapest:** Needs minimal fertilizer

---

### SWEET POTATOES (6,000 KSh/ha)
- **Fertilizer 1:** DAP (150-200 kg)
- **Fertilizer 2:** Potassium Chloride (100-150 kg)
- **Vines:** 25,000 disease-free cuttings
- **Manure:** 10-15 tons/ha
- **Mulch:** 5-8 tons

---

### SORGHUM (3,500 KSh/ha) - DROUGHT TOLERANT
- **Fertilizer:** CAN (150-200 kg)
- **Seed:** 12-15 kg/ha improved seed
- **Manure:** 5-8 tons/ha
- **Pesticide:** Only if armyworms appear

**Advantage:** Drought tolerant, needs less water

---

### RICE (9,500 KSh/ha) - FOR WET AREAS
- **Fertilizer 1:** NPK 17:17:17 (300-350 kg)
- **Fertilizer 2:** Urea (200-250 kg)
- **Seed:** 40-50 kg/ha certified seed
- **Pesticide 1:** For brown plant hopper (if appears)
- **Pesticide 2:** Mancozeb (every 10-14 days)

## Save Money

### Strategy 1: Group Buying
Join farmer group → Buy together → Save 15-25%

### Strategy 2: Use Manure
Skip half the fertilizer → Use farmyard manure → Save 30-40%

### Strategy 3: Organic Pests
Use neem oil, soap spray → Skip expensive pesticides → Save 25-35%

### Strategy 4: Mulch Everything
Use farm waste → Reduce weeding/watering → Save 20-30%

### Strategy 5: Smart Spraying
Only spray if you SEE pests → Don't spray on schedule → Save 25-35%

## Application Timing

### At Planting
✓ Base fertilizer (NPK)
✓ Soil amendments (Manure, Lime)
✓ Seeds/Seedlings
✓ Soil insecticide (if needed)

### 4-6 Weeks After Planting
✓ Top dressing (CAN)
✓ First pesticide spray (if pests)

### 8-10 Weeks After Planting
✓ Second top dressing (Urea if maize)
✓ Regular pesticide sprays (if disease/pests)

### Flowering & Fruiting
✓ Potassium fertilizer
✓ Intensive pesticide schedule

### Before Harvest
⚠️ STOP spraying 2 weeks before harvest (check label)

---

## Safety Rules

### ALWAYS
✓ Wear gloves + mask + hat
✓ Spray early morning or evening
✓ Keep children away
✓ Store in cool, dark place
✓ Keep away from water sources
✓ Wash hands after handling
✓ Read label carefully

### NEVER
✗ Spray on windy days
✗ Eat while handling chemicals
✗ Spray near water
✗ Exceed recommended dose
✗ Spray within harvest window
✗ Keep chemicals with food
✗ Leave chemicals in sun

**POISONED? Get to hospital immediately!**

## Before You Plant

### One Month Before
- [ ] Contact agricultural office
- [ ] Join farmer group (if possible)
- [ ] Test soil or ask extension officer
- [ ] Order quality seeds
- [ ] Start buying fertilizers

### Two Weeks Before
- [ ] Prepare land
- [ ] Gather/prepare manure
- [ ] Check all tools
- [ ] Plan input application schedule
- [ ] Arrange transport for heavy inputs

### At Planting
- [ ] Apply base fertilizer
- [ ] Apply soil amendments
- [ ] Plant quality seeds
- [ ] Water if dry
- [ ] Mulch if possible

---

## During Growing Season

### Every 3-4 Days
- [ ] Scout for pests/diseases
- [ ] Check if water is adequate
- [ ] Check for nutrient deficiency (yellowing)

### When You See Problems
- Go to agricultural office with leaf sample
- Get expert advice before spraying
- Don't spray on guesswork

### Keep Records
- Note what you applied and cost
- Note when you applied
- Record harvest yield
- Use data for next season

## Common Problems & Solutions

| Problem | Cause | Solution | Cost |
|---------|-------|----------|------|
| Yellow leaves | Low nitrogen | Add CAN or manure | 2,800 KSh/bag |
| Purple leaves | Low phosphorus | Add DAP | 3,200 KSh/bag |
| Brown spots | Fungal disease | Spray Mancozeb | 1,200 KSh/kg |
| Holes in leaves | Insects | Spray Cypermethrin | 800 KSh/L |
| Wilting | Not enough water | Water or mulch | 500-1,500 KSh |
| Poor germination | Bad seeds | Buy certified seeds | 350-450 KSh/kg |
| Tall but no yield | Too much nitrogen | Skip second fertilizer | Save 2,800 KSh |

## Organic Options
